YALA NATIONAL PARK


Yala National Park is situated in south east region of Sri lanka. The entrance to the part is 12km from kirinda and is known as Palatupana. There are other parks like Kumana and wilpattu, but Yala is the best place to witness a diversity of wildlife.

BUNDALA NATIONAL PARK

The most important birding destination in Sri Lanka is the Bundala National Park. It is located in the southern province. It was declared as a sanctuary and a national park in yeat 1969 and 1993 subsequently. It is reachale from many tourist destinations.
